Introduction

EA Sports FC 24 is a football simulation video game created by EA Vancouver and EA Romania, and published by EA Sports.

Basic rules

This game adheres to all official football rules, from tracking offside players to allowing mid-game substitutions. It enforces fouls and penalties ensuring fairplay. Moreover, the attention to detail in gameplay mechanics elevates the overall enjoyment, bringing the excitement of football right to your screen.

How to start?

This game is versatile and can be played on consoles, PCs, and even smartphones. At the start, you are given a squad of players who will help you win matches. As you triumph on the field, you earn valuable collectibles(coins and gems) that can be exchanged for better players, allowing you to continuously enhance and strengthen your team.

Events

There are different events held in EAFC-24. Some of them are:-

  • Team of the Year:- Participate in matches to win players who have delivered exceptional performances over the past year.
  • Heroes:- Relive the glory days by winning matches to acquire iconic players who have graced the game in the past.
  • Winter Wildcards:- Compete in matches to earn a diverse range of players, from legendary figures to today's stars.
